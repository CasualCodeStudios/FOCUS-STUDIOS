Birthday Party Coverage

Full event photography for birthday celebrations of all sizes — from intimate family gatherings to large themed parties. We capture every laugh, hug, and cake-cutting moment.

  • Candid and posed portraits
  • Cake cutting and key moments

Baby Shower Photography

Tender, emotive coverage of baby shower celebrations. We document the joy of expecting — from beautiful bump portraits to the warmth shared among loved ones.
