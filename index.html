Frequently Asked Questions

Do you travel outside Nairobi for shoots?
Yes. We travel across Kenya for weddings, events, portraits and corporate assignments. Travel fees may apply depending on location, and we'll include them in your quote upfront with no hidden costs.
How soon will we receive our photos?
Your edited photos are delivered within 24–48 hours after the shoot. For larger weddings or commercial projects, we share the full gallery within 5–7 business days. Rush delivery options are available on request.
Can we book a consultation before our session?
Absolutely. Every booking includes a planning call so we can discuss locations, styling, pacing and any special requests before your shoot. We want to ensure every detail is perfect before we even pick up a camera.
Do you offer packages for corporate and branding work?
Yes. We offer custom packages for corporate headshots, product photography, events and branding campaigns. Contact us with your brief and we'll create a tailored proposal that fits your brand and budget.
What should I wear for my portrait session?
We recommend solid, neutral colours or classic patterns. Avoid logos and overly bright colours. We send a full preparation guide after booking — it covers outfits, locations, timing and what to expect on the day.
How do you handle outdoor shoots when weather is bad?
We monitor weather closely and will reach out 24 hours in advance if conditions look unfavourable. We offer free rescheduling for weather-related cancellations and can pivot to covered or studio locations at short notice.
Can I order prints or albums from my gallery?
Yes. We partner with premium print labs in Nairobi to offer fine-art prints, canvas wraps and custom photo albums. You can order directly through your online gallery once your images are delivered.
Do you shoot videos as well as photos?
Yes! We offer videography services including highlight reels, cinematic films and event coverage. Ask about our bundled photo + video packages for the best value when booking your wedding or event.
