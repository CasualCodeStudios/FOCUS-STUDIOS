3-Stage Production Process

How It Works

From Brief to
Finished Film

Great video isn't made on the day of the shoot — it's built across three carefully managed stages. Every project we take on goes through this process, whether it's a 90-second social reel or a full wedding feature film.

Stage 01 · Pre-Production
Discovery & Creative Planning

We start with a detailed brief — understanding your vision, audience, message, and must-have moments. From this we develop a shot list, run-of-show schedule, location plan, talent and equipment requirements, and a production timeline. Nothing reaches camera day without a plan.

Stage 02 · Production
Shoot Day — Capturing the Film

Our camera teams shoot with cinema-grade Sony, Canon, and DJI equipment across multiple cameras and angles. Audio is captured with professional wireless lavalier and directional microphone systems. Drone flights are handled by a licensed remote pilot where applicable. Every card is backed up on-site before we leave.

Stage 03 · Post-Production
Edit, Grade & Deliver

Footage is logged, organised and edited in Adobe Premiere Pro. Colour grading in DaVinci Resolve brings the visual tone to life. Licensed music is synchronised and mixed with location audio. You receive a first cut for review, one round of revisions, and the final approved export in all required formats. Delivery is via private link and USB.

Stage 04 · Distribution
Platform-Ready Exports

We don't just hand you one file. Every project is exported in formats optimised for your specific platforms — broadcast-grade H.264/H.265 masters, YouTube-ready uploads, compressed social media versions, vertical 9:16 cuts for Reels and TikTok, and compressed WhatsApp-ready previews. Your film works everywhere.

Your Questions, Answered

Frequently Asked

Everything you need to know before booking a video project with Focus Studios. Honest, detailed answers — no sales fluff.

How far in advance should I book?
+
For weddings and large events, we recommend booking at least 3–6 months in advance, particularly for peak season (December, August, and long weekends). For corporate and commercial projects, 3–6 weeks typically allows for proper pre-production planning. We do accommodate short-notice bookings where our schedule allows — reach out via WhatsApp for availability checks.
Do you provide drone footage?
+
Yes. We operate DJI Mavic 3 Pro drones flown by a licensed remote pilot in command. Drone footage is included in our Signature and Cinematic packages, and available as an add-on for Standard. Note that drone operations are subject to Kenya Civil Aviation Authority (KCAA) regulations — certain urban areas, airspace near airports, and national parks require permits that we arrange in advance. There are a small number of locations where drones cannot legally fly; we will advise you during the booking process.
Can I choose the music for my film?
+
Absolutely. We maintain a licensed music library spanning cinematic orchestral, Afrobeats, gospel, contemporary pop, and ambient genres. You can browse and select from our library, or request a genre/mood and we'll suggest tracks. If you have a specific song in mind, we'll do our best to source a licensed version. Note: unlicensed popular music cannot be used in videos intended for YouTube or social media publication — we will always advise on this to protect your content from copyright strikes.
How many cameras do you use?
+
Our Standard package uses a single cinema camera with a gimbal operator. Signature and Cinematic packages use dual-camera setups — one primary camera operator and one secondary/roaming operator — plus drone where applicable. For large events and weddings, we can deploy up to three ground cameras simultaneously. Multi-camera coverage ensures that the same moment is never missed and gives our editors richer material to work with during the cut.
What is the turnaround time for editing?
+
Standard turnaround is 7–10 business days for highlight films. Full footage archive delivery takes 3–5 additional days. Social media cuts are typically delivered within 2–3 days of final film approval. Rush delivery (within 48 hours) is available as a premium add-on for urgent projects. Wedding films follow the same timeline — a teaser clip (60–90 seconds) can be delivered within 48 hours as an optional add-on for sharing on social media while you wait for the full edit.
Do you shoot outside Nairobi?
+
Yes — we travel across Kenya. We regularly shoot in Mombasa, Nyeri, Nakuru, Kisumu, Eldoret, and remote locations. Travel costs (transport, accommodation where required, and per diem) are quoted separately based on the shoot location. For international shoots — particularly destination weddings or corporate projects — we are also available with advance booking and appropriate travel arrangements. WhatsApp us with your location and dates for a custom quote.
